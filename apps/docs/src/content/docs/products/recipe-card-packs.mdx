Recipe card packs are sets of individually formatted recipe cards, one per page, designed for printing. They are a simple, popular product format that works well as both paid products and lead magnets.

## Creating a recipe card pack

1. Go to **Products** > **New Product** > **Recipe Card Pack**.
2. Select recipes from your library or choose a collection.
3. Choose the card size: **4 x 6** (index card size) or **5 x 7** (slightly larger).
4. Preview the cards. Each card shows the primary photo, title, cook time, ingredients, and instructions.
5. Click **Generate** to render the multi-page PDF.

## What each card includes

- Primary photo
- Recipe title
- Cook time and yield
- Complete ingredient list
- Complete instructions
- Dietary tag icons (if confirmed)

<Aside type="tip">
  Recipe card packs make excellent lead magnets. Choose your top 3-5 recipes by engagement score and
  offer the pack as a free download to grow your list.
</Aside>

Cards are styled with your brand kit and rendered as a multi-page PDF (one card per page).
