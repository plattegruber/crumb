Once your product is complete and all AI copy has been reviewed, you can publish it to one or more sales platforms. crumb handles the upload and listing creation.

## Supported platforms

| Platform       | Integration                        |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Stan Store** | API or credential-based connection |
| **Gumroad**    | OAuth connection                   |
| **LTK**        | API key connection                 |

## Publishing flow

1. Open a completed product and click **Publish**.
2. Select the destination platform(s).
3. Set a price. This is display-only — crumb does not process payments. The actual transaction happens on the sales platform.
4. Click **Publish**. crumb uploads the PDF and creates a listing on the platform.
5. When the listing is live, crumb stores the listing URL on your product record.

If the product has an associated lead magnet, crumb automatically updates the lead magnet's Day 7 pitch email with the listing URL.

<Aside>
  If a platform's API is unavailable or you have not connected an account, crumb packages your files
  into a ZIP and gives you a download link with manual upload instructions.
</Aside>

## Social share assets

When you publish a product, crumb generates three share-ready images:

- **Square** (1080 x 1080) — for Instagram feed posts.
- **Vertical** (1080 x 1920) — for TikTok or Reels cover images.
- **Story** (1080 x 1920 with safe zones) — for Instagram or TikTok Stories.

Each image includes your brand color, product cover or primary recipe photo, product title, and a call-to-action ("Get it free" for lead magnets, "Available now" for paid products).

Download these from your product dashboard and share on social media.

<Aside type="tip">
  Publishing to multiple platforms lets your audience buy where they are most comfortable. Many
  creators publish the same product on both Stan Store and Gumroad.
</Aside>
