On the Studio tier, you can invite up to 3 team members to your crumb account. Team members share access to your recipe library, products, and analytics dashboard.

## Inviting a team member

1. Go to **Settings** > **Team**.
2. Click **Invite Member**.
3. Enter their email address.
4. They receive an invitation email with a link to accept.

## What team members can do

- Browse and edit recipes in the library.
- Create and edit products.
- View analytics and segmentation data.
- Insert recipe cards in Kit emails (using the shared Kit connection).

## What team members cannot do

- Disconnect the Kit integration.
- Change billing or subscription tier.
- Delete the account.
- Invite or remove other team members (only the account owner can).

<Aside>
  Team member access is revocable at any time. Go to **Settings** > **Team** and click **Remove** next to any member.
</Aside>
