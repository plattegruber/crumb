dough offers four tiers to match your needs as you grow. Every account starts on the Free tier.

## Free tier limits

The Free tier includes the full feature set with usage limits:

- **25 active recipes** — you can have up to 25 recipes in Draft or Active status. Archived recipes do not count.
- **3 recipe card email sends per month** — you can mark up to 3 recipes as email-ready per calendar month.
- **1 published product** — you can have one product in Published status at a time.

These are hard limits enforced by the application. When you hit a limit, dough shows a clear message explaining what you need to upgrade for.

<Aside type="tip">
  The Free tier is fully functional for trying out dough and sending your first few recipe emails.
  Upgrade when you need more volume.
</Aside>

## Studio tier extras

The Studio tier adds:

- **Multiple brand kits** — create different visual identities for different product lines.
- **Team members** — invite up to 3 team members who share access to your library, products, and analytics. Team members cannot change billing, disconnect Kit, or delete the account.
