A lead magnet is a free sample generated from an existing product. dough automates the entire process: selecting the best recipes, creating a condensed PDF, building a Kit opt-in form, and setting up a delivery sequence that nurtures new subscribers toward purchasing the full product.

## How lead magnet generation works

1. Open a published (or draft) ebook or recipe card pack.
2. Click **Generate Lead Magnet**.
3. dough selects the top **3-5 recipes** from the product by engagement score. If engagement scores are not available, the first 5 recipes are used.
4. A condensed PDF is rendered using the same template as the parent product.
5. The last page of the PDF includes a call to action: "Get the full [Product Title]" with a link to the product's listing (if published).
6. dough creates a **Kit opt-in form** that includes a dietary preference multi-select field.
7. dough creates a **Kit delivery sequence** (see below).

## The delivery sequence

When a lead magnet is created, dough sets up a 4-email Kit sequence:

| Day   | Email                                                                       |
| ----- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **0** | Delivery — "Here's your free [Lead Magnet Title]!" with the PDF attached.   |
| **2** | Value email — tips related to the lead magnet topic (AI-drafted, you edit). |
| **4** | Another recipe card from your library.                                      |
| **7** | Pitch — "Get the full [Product Title]" with a link to the sales page.       |

All sequence emails are created as drafts. You review and activate the sequence in Kit.

<Aside type="caution">
  You must review and activate the sequence in Kit before it will start sending. dough creates the
  drafts but does not activate them automatically.
</Aside>

## The opt-in form

The generated Kit form:

- Captures email and first name.
- Includes a multi-select field for dietary preferences (Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free, Vegan, Vegetarian, Keto, Paleo, Nut-Free).
- Tags subscribers on sign-up for tracking.

The form embed code is shown in your dough dashboard. Embed it on your blog, Substack, or landing page.

<Aside type="tip">
  The dietary preference field on the opt-in form feeds directly into your audience segmentation.
  Every lead magnet download becomes a data point for understanding your audience.
</Aside>
